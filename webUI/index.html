How Our Story Began










At Malpani Mangoes our mission is to help farmers get the best value for their products and our customers get authentic and natural produce directly from the people who grow it.

Devgad is known for the worlds best mangoes but its growers(the farmers) are still not able to reach the cities in india and consumers too don’t get the original “Devgad” because they are lost in the market full of dummy mangoes. Farmers spend enormous efforts in Plucking, Grading ,Packing , and transportation of mangoes during the scorching heat of summer. So they don’t connect with the customers in the limited time and even if they do , customers bargain big time with farmers demotivating them in doing sales in the Indian cities. We are also committed to stop the harassment of farmers by cutting the middlemen and give the maximum share of profit in farmers hand.
